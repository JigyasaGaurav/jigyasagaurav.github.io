Hi, I'm Jigyasa

I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, advised by Prof. Aryan Deshwal.

My research focuses on uncertainty-aware Bayesian optimization algorithms for discovering optimal designs in large offline design spaces — with applications in molecule discovery, protein engineering, and materials design.

My background spans pure mathematics (combinatorics, graph theory, differential topology) at IIT Bombay and IISc Bangalore, and two years engineering deep learning systems in computer vision and NLP at V-Labs. Outside research: avid badminton player, former theater member at IISc's Rangmanch, and enthusiastic teacher.
